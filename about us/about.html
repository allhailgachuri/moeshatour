About Us

Safari Market, a Tours & Safaris Company with its headquarters in Nairobi, Kenya, started in 1997 with an average of 300 visitors per month from the USA, Europe, Japan, and South Africa. We specialize in Camping Safaris, Luxury Lodge Safaris, Mountain Climbing, Car Hire, Hotel and Lodge Bookings, and International and Domestic Air Tickets in both Kenya and Tanzania.

We have a team of competent and experienced staff with not less than 5 years in the tourism industry.

All our safari driver/guides are well knowledgeable about animals, birds, people, and the country as a whole. We put our clients first; therefore, we listen to their needs and budgets. With our extensive knowledge about safaris, we are able to create an itinerary to make a dream holiday come true.

Our transportation consists of safari-designed vehicles, either 2WD or 4WD, ranging from 3-seater cars to 25-seater microbuses.

Most of our vehicles are fitted with radio communication and have pop-up roofs for easy photography and sighting of animals.
