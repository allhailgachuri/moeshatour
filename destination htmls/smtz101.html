4 DAYS MANYARA / NGORONGORO / TARANGIRE

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / L.MANYARA Depart Nairobi at 0830hrs and drive to Arusha for lunch. Proceed to L.Manyara National Park on the base of the Great Rift Valley. This small park contains five distinct vegetation zones, thus supporting a large variety of birds and animals. The park is also famous for its tree-climbing lions and large flocks of flamingoes. Dinner and overnight at the lodge or campsite.

DAY 2 - L.MANYARA / NGORONGORO CRATER After breakfast, leave for Ngorongoro. Taking a picnic lunch with you, descend down to the crater floor 2000ft for game viewing. Break for lunch at Ngoitoktok Springs picnic site for an unforgettable experience under the sun. Ascend late afternoon for dinner and overnight at the lodge or campsite.

DAY 3 - NGORONGORO / TARANGIRE After breakfast, drive to Tarangire National Park. Lunch at the campsite or lodge then relax till 1600hrs when you go out for the evening game drive up to 1800hrs. Return to the lodge or campsite for dinner and overnight.

DAY 4 - TARANGIRE / NAIROBI After breakfast, depart for Arusha arriving in time for lunch. Depart for Nairobi at 1400hrs to arrive early evening.
