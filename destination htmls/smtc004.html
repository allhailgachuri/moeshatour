- 4 DAYS MASAI MARA / L. NAKURU

Itinerary

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / MASAI MARA 0930HRS:

Depart Nairobi for Masai Mara. Travel through the edge of the Rift Valley, stopping at the viewpoint for photography. Lunch at Narok town. Arrive at Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive from 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 2 - MASAI MARA:

Full day to be spent in this spectacular game reserve, where all the big five can be spotted. A picnic lunch will be prepared and packed by our cook for the full-day game drive. Depart the camp at 0830hrs after a full breakfast and spend the whole day in the park, including a visit to the hippos and crocodile pool. Return to the camp late afternoon at 1500hrs. Rest of the day at leisure, with dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 3 - MASAI MARA / NAIROBI:

Early morning game drives from 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp, then leave for Lake Nakuru with lunch en route, arriving late afternoon. Dinner and overnight at a hotel in town.

DAY 4 - LAKE NAKURU / NAIROBI:

After breakfast, depart for a half-day game drive in this park, famous for the millions of pink flamingos and other species of birds. Depart after lunch for Nairobi, arriving late afternoon.
