- 4 DAYS AMBOSELI / TSAVO WEST

Itinerary

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / AMBOSELI 0930HRS:

Depart Nairobi for Amboseli National Park with picnic lunch at Namanga border post. On arrival, proceed for a game drive in search of the big five with the magnificent Mt. Kilimanjaro in the background. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 2 - AMBOSELI:

Full day to be spent in the park. Early morning game drives at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Breakfast at the campsite, then a further game drive at 1000hrs. Lunch at the campsite, then relax at the camp until late afternoon at 1600hrs for an evening game drive until 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 3 - TSAVO WEST:

Breakfast at the campsite, then leave for Tsavo West, arriving in time for lunch. Proceed for an evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 4 - TSAVO WEST / NAIROBI:

Early morning game drives at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Breakfast at the camp, then leave for Nairobi with a picnic lunch en route. Arrive late afternoon.
