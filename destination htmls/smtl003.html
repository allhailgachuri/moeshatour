3 DAYS MASAI MARA

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / MASAI MARA 0930HRS: Depart Nairobi for a journey south along the floor of the Rift Valley stopping at the viewpoint for photographing. Lunch at Narok town then climb the western wall entering Hemingway's country "Serengeti plains". Arrive Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 2 - MASI MARA: Full day to be spent in this spectacular game reserve. All the big five can be spotted. Picnic lunch will be prepared and packed by our cook in readiness for the full day game drive. Depart the camp at 0830hrs after full breakfast and spend the whole day in the park with a visit to the hippos and crocodiles pool. Return to the camp late afternoon at 1500hrs. Rest of the day at leisure, dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 3 - MASAI MARA / NAIROBI: Early morning game drive at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp then leave for Nairobi with lunch enroute to arrive late afternoon.
