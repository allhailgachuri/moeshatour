SMA 001 - One Night Amboseli

DAY 1 - Nairobi / Amboseli

Departure from hotel at 0630hrs to Wilson Airstrip. Fly at 0730hrs to arrive Amboseli at 0815hrs and proceed for a full game drive. Lunch at the lodge then relax in the afternoon. Evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

DAY 2 - Amboseli / Nairobi

Breakfast at the lodge then transfer to the airstrip for flight at 0730hrs. Arrive Nairobi at 0830hrs, transfer to the hotel.

SMA 003 - One Night Masai Mara (A.M - A.M Package)

DAY 1 - Nairobi / Masai Mara

Pick up from your hotel at 0900hrs and transfer to Wilson Airstrip. Fly to the Masai Mara at 1000hrs arriving at 1045hrs. Transfer to the lodge then a game drive to be provided. Lunch at the lodge. Evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs, dinner and overnight at the lodge/Tented Camp.

DAY 2 - Masai Mara / Nairobi

Early morning game drive at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Breakfast at the lodge then Transfer to the airstrip for a morning flight to Nairobi at 1100hrs arriving at 1215hrs. Transfer to your hotel.

SMA 007 - Two Nights Lamu

DAY 1 - Nairobi / Lamu

Pick up from your hotel at 1200Noon and transfer to the airstrip for flight to Lamu at 1315hrs arriving at 1445hrs. Boat transfer to your hotel then rest of the day at leisure at the beach. Dinner and overnight at a Peponi Hotel or the Island Hotel.

DAY 2 - Lamu

Full day at leisure. Accommodation on Half board basis (breakfast and dinner only) at the Island hotel but Full board basis at Peponi hotel.

DAY 3 - Lamu / Nairobi

Breakfast at the hotel then half day at leisure. Transfer to the airstrip for flight to Nairobi at 1545hrs arriving at 1715hrs. Transfer to your hotel.
