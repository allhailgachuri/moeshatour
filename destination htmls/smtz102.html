6 DAYS NGORONGORO / SERENGETI / MANYARA

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / NGORONGORO Depart Nairobi at 0830hrs for Arusha through Namanga border, arriving at 1300hrs. Lunch at Arusha then leave for Ngorongoro crater, arriving in time for dinner and overnight at the campsite or the lodge.

DAY 2 - NGORONGORO Descend down the crater 2000ft with picnic lunches for a full day crater tour. Ascend late afternoon for dinner and overnight.

DAY 3 - NGORONGORO / SERENGETI Leave after breakfast for Serengeti National Park via Olduvai Gorge. Lunch at the campsite or lodge. Evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs, then dinner and overnight.

DAY 4 - SERENGETI Early morning game drive at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. This game drive proves rewarding because it is very fresh and lively. Breakfast at the campsite or lodge. After breakfast, game drive with picnic lunches to search for the big five. Return in the evening for dinner and overnight.

DAY 5 - SERENGETI / L.MANYARA Leave after breakfast for Manyara to arrive in time for lunch. Evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the camp or lodge.

DAY 6 - L.MANYARA / ARUSHA / NAIROBI Early morning game drive, then after breakfast, depart for Arusha, arriving in time for lunch. Depart for Nairobi in the afternoon.
