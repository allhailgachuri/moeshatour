3 DAYS AMBOSELI

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / AMBOSELI 0930HRS:

Depart Nairobi for Amboseli National Park with a picnic lunch at Namanga border post. On arrival, proceed for a game drive in search of the big five with the magnificent Mt. Kilimanjaro in the background. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 2 - AMBOSELI

Full day in the park. Early morning game drive from 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Breakfast at the campsite followed by a game drive at 1000hrs. Lunch at the campsite, then relax until a late afternoon game drive at 1600hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 3 - AMBOSELI / NAIROBI

Early morning game drive from 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp, then leave for Nairobi with a picnic lunch enroute. Arrive in Nairobi in the late afternoon.
