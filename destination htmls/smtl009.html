- 11 DAYS GRAND KENYA

DAY 1 TO DAY 6 SAME AS SMT C 007.

DAY 7 - SAMBURU / MT. KENYA Early morning game drive at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast then leave for Mt.Kenya Lunch at Nanyuki then afternoon hike up to the first camp up the mountain with a guide or visit the Mau Mau caves where freedom fighters used to hide. Dinner and overnight at a hotel in Nanyuki town.

DAY 8 - MT.KENYA / NAIROBI / AMBOSELI After breakfast leave for Amboseli with lunch at Nairobi. Arrive in the evening in time for dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 9 - AMBOSELI Full day game viewing in this park with the majestic snow capped Mt.Kilimanjaro (Africa's highest mountain) in the background. All meals and overnight at the camp.

DAY 10 - AMBOSELI / TSAVO WEST Early morning game drive in Amboseli. Leave for Tsavo West arriving in time for lunch. Evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 11 - TSAVO WEST / NAIROBI Early morning game drive at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Breakfast at the camp then leave for Nairobi with picnic lunch enroute. Arrive late afternoon.

  • Tanzania :- 1 | 2 | 3
