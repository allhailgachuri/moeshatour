6 DAYS MASAI MARA / L.NAKURU / L.BOGORIA / L.BARINGO

DAY 1 - NAIROBI / MASAI MARA 0930HRS: Depart Nairobi for Masai Mara. Travel through the edge of the Rift Valley, stopping at the viewpoint for photographing. Lunch at Narok town. Arrive Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 2 - MASAI MARA: Full day to be spent in this spectacular game reserve. All the big five can be spotted. Picnic lunch will be prepared and packed by our cook in readiness for the full day game drive. Depart the camp at 0830hrs after a full breakfast and spend the whole day in the park with a visit to the hippos and crocodiles pool. Return to the camp late afternoon at 1500hrs. Rest of the day at leisure, dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 3 - Another full day in Masai Mara: A nature walk shall be provided by a Masai guide in the park. This is also a good day to visit a Masai village to see their lifestyle and dance. Alternatively, game drives can be provided. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 4 - MASAI MARA / L.NAKURU: Early morning game drives at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp then leave for L.Nakuru with lunch en route to arrive late afternoon, dinner, and overnight at a hotel in town.

DAY 5 - L.NAKURU / L.BARINGO: After breakfast, depart for a half-day game drive in this park famous for the millions of pink flamingoes and other species of birds. Depart after lunch for L.Baringo. Those who prefer can organize a boat trip to view the many birds that inhabit this lake at an extra cost. There's also camel riding and a remote Njemp's village, which can be done. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

DAY 6 - L.BARINGO / L.BOGORIA / NAIROBI: Explore on foot this naturalist lake and view the hot water springs and the lesser flamingoes in their thousands. Caution, however, must be taken while walking near the hot springs as they are super hot. Depart for Nairobi with lunch en route to arrive in the evening.
