- 15 DAYS BEST OF KENYA

Day 1 - Nairobi / Masai Mara 0930HRS:

Depart Nairobi for Masai Mara. Travel through the edge of the Rift Valley stopping at the viewpoint for photographing. Lunch at Narok town. Arrive Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

Day 2 and Day 3 - Masai Mara

Two full days in the park. Game drives in our special 9-seater safari bus. All meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 4 - Masai Mara / L.Nakuru

Early morning game drives at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp then leave for L.Nakuru with lunch en route to arrive late afternoon, dinner and overnight at a hotel in town.

Day 5 - L.Nakuru / L.Naivasha

Early morning game drive at L.Nakuru then leave for L.Naivasha after breakfast. Lunch at the camp. Rest of the day at leisure boating to the Crescent Island or a nature walk.

Day 6 - L.Naivasha / Nairobi / Amboseli

Breakfast then leave for Amboseli via Nairobi. Lunch at Namanga border post. Depart after lunch to arrive in Amboseli in time for a game drive in the evening. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 7 - Amboseli

Full day game viewing in this park with the majestic snow-capped Mt.Kilimanjaro (Africa's highest mountain) in the background. All meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 8 - Amboseli / Tsavo West

Early morning game drive in Amboseli. Leave for Tsavo West arriving in time for lunch. Evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 9 - Tsavo West / Mombasa

Early morning game drive. Breakfast then another game drive to Mzima Springs where hippos, crocodiles, and fresh water fish are seen swimming in the clear waters. Return to the camp for lunch, leave for Mombasa arriving in the evening for dinner and overnight at Bamburi beach hotel or similar.

Day 10, Day 11, Day 12 - Mombasa

3 full days at the beach side on a half-board basis.

Day 13 - Independent Sports

Such as swimming, snorkeling, diving, surfing, etc.

Day 14 - Mombasa / Nairobi

Full day at leisure till 5.00 p.m. Transfer to the railway station at 1730hrs to board an overnight train to Nairobi to arrive the next day at 0900hrs.
