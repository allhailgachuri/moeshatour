- 5 DAYS MASAI MARA / L. NAKURU

Itinerary

Day 1 to Day 2: Same as SMT C 004. Depart Nairobi for Masai Mara. Travel through the edge of the Rift Valley, stopping at the viewpoint for photography. Lunch at Narok town. Arrive at Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive from 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

Day 3: Another full day in Masai Mara. A nature walk will be provided by a Masai guide in the park. This day is also suitable for visiting a Masai village to experience their lifestyle and dance. Alternatively, game drives can be arranged. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 4 - MASAI MARA / L. NAKURU:

Early morning game drives from 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp, then leave for Lake Nakuru with lunch en route, arriving late afternoon. Dinner and overnight at a hotel in town.

Day 5 - LAKE NAKURU / NAIROBI:

After breakfast, depart for a half-day game drive in the park. Depart after lunch for Nairobi, arriving late afternoon.
