7 DAYS MASAI MARA / L.NAKURU / SAMBURU

Day 1 - Nairobi / Masai Mara 0930HRS:

Depart Nairobi for Masai Mara. Travel through the edge of the Rift Valley stopping at the viewpoint for photographing. Lunch at Narok town. Arrive Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive at 1600hrs to 1800hrs. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

Day 2 - Masai Mara

Full day to be spent in this spectacular game reserve. All the big five can be spotted. Picnic lunch will be prepared and packed by our cook in readiness for the full day game drive. Depart the camp at 0830hrs after full breakfast and spend the whole day in the park with a visit to the hippos and crocodiles pool. Return to the camp late afternoon at 1500hrs. Rest of the day at leisure, dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 3 - Another full day in Masai Mara.

A nature walk shall be provided by a Masai guide in the park. This also is a good day to visit a Masai village to see their lifestyle and dance. Alternatively, game drives can be provided. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 4 - Masai Mara / L.Nakuru

Early morning game drives at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast at the camp then leave for L.Nakuru with lunch en route to arrive late afternoon, dinner and overnight at a hotel in town.

Day 5 - L.Nakuru / Samburu

Pre-breakfast game drive in L.Nakuru then leave for Samburu after breakfast. Lunch en route arriving in time for an evening game drive in Samburu. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 6 - Samburu

Full day to be spent in this game reserve famous for its gravy zebras and reticulated giraffes. All meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 7 - Samburu / Mt.Kenya / Nairobi

Early morning game drives at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Full breakfast then leave for Nairobi via Mt.Kenya for lunch. Clients who wish to climb Mt. Kenya can be dropped here and the rest to leave for Nairobi arriving late afternoon.
