SIRIMON ROUTE 5 DAYS

DAY 1: NAIROBI - NANYUKI. Leave Nairobi for Nanyuki by public means accompanied by our mountain guide arriving in time for lunch. Spend the afternoon acclimatizing. Dinner and overnight at a hotel in Nanyuki.

DAY 2: NANYUKI - OLD MOSES. Transfer from your Nanyuki hotel after breakfast to the Sirimon gate where the climbing starts. Walk through the rain forest for 9 KM (5 hours walk) to the first mountain hut - Old Moses (3,300m Above the sea level). Dinner and overnight at the hut.

DAY 3: OLD MOSES - SHIPTON CAMP. After breakfast at Old Moses camp, continue climbing through Mackinders Valley to the second mountain hut - Shipton hut (4,200m) for dinner and overnight.

DAY 4: SHIPTON HUT - OLD MOSES. Early in the morning you ascend to point Lenana 4,985m (17,058 ft). On a very clear day you should be able to see Mount Kilimanjaro to the south, Mount Elgon to the west, and Indian Ocean to the east. Descend to Shipton camp for breakfast, and soon after descend to Old Moses for dinner and overnight.

DAY 5: OLD MOSES - NAIROBI. After breakfast transfer to Old Moses to catch transport to Nairobi where the tour ENDS!

  • Mount Kilimanjaro :- 1 | 2 | 3
