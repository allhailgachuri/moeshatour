5 Days Nairobi Airport - Masai Mara - Nairobi Airport

Itinerary Overview

DAY 1 - NAIROBI AIRPORT - HOSTEL/GUEST HOUSE

Pick up from Nairobi's JKIA airport free of charge and transfer to a budget hotel/Guest house or a Youth hostel for overnight stay on Bed and Breakfast basis.

DAY 2 - NAIROBI - MASAI MARA

Pick up after breakfast and travel through the Rift Valley stopping at the view point. Lunch at Narok town or Picnic box. Arrive Masai Mara in time for an evening game drive. Dinner and overnight at the Campsite.

DAY 3 - MASAI MARA

Full day in Masai Mara. Breakfast at 0700hrs then leave for a full day game drive with picnic lunches. Return to the camp before sunset for dinner and overnight at the campsite.

DAY 4 - MASAI MARA - NAIROBI

Early morning game drive at 0630hrs to 0830hrs. Breakfast at the campsite then leave for Nairobi with picnic lunch enroute. Overnight at a Guest house/ Hostel or budget hotel.

DAY 5 - NAIROBI - AIRPORT

Breakfast then rest of the day at leisure. Shopping for souvenirs, city tour or visit the Museum. Transfer to the airport for flight back home.
